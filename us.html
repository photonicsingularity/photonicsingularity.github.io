Why you should join and our goals

Our Goals

In the 21th century, the rise of the internet and social media has made politics, news, and global affairs more available to the younger generation. Our club not only encourages students to learn and understand more about the politics of the world around us and how it all works and is run, but also to actively participate and practice in important life skills, such as public speaking, research skills, and networking. But most importantly, we want to have fun!

Why should I join?

There are many reasons to join not only Colonel By Model United Nations club, but Model United Nations in general. Joining Model United Nations as an extracurricular and going to prestigious High School level and University level conferences will be useful for university applications, and will provide very valuable social experiences and contacts. Students will also develop speaking, leadership, networking, and social skills, and will be on the way to becoming a global citizen. Also, if you are in the International Baccalaureate program in Colonel By, CBMUN will count towards the creative section of CAS hours.
