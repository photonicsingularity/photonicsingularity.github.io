Lisgar 2017 MUN Conference - December 1st

 

Lisgar Model UN is hosting their 2017-2018 Interschool Conference in the Lisgar library on Thursday, December 1st from 9:00 AM to 2:30 PM. This year will be split into two committees, the Arctic Council and the United Nations Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space (UNCPUOS), in the East and West Library rooms respectively.

 

Arctic Council (a non-UN body) consists of observers and indigenous associations on top of the regular member-states. Indigenous associations hold membership status too. Observer states can act as signatories, participate in debate and motion but CANNOT vote or introduce resolutions.

UNCOPOUS contains some private space corporations who are also observer states. Once again, companies like SpaceX can participate in debate and motion but CANNOT vote or introduce resolutions.

 

The Arctic Council will be discussing the regulation of both international boundaries and private companies within the context of the Arctic while the UNCPUOS will be setting a framework for space colonization, regulation, and privatization.

 

Lunch will be between 11:25 AM and 12:25 PM. There are plenty of places to buy a lunch along Elgin and people are welcome to bring their own and eat in the cafeteria too. We will be providing small refreshments during the conference.
